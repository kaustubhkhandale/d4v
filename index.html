About Sunn Grahak

Empowering Sindhi Businesses, Connecting Communities

At Sunn Grahak, we take immense pride in introducing ourselves as the driving force behind India's exclusive Sindhi Business Directory. Our app is more than just a marketplace; it's a digital haven meticulously crafted to empower Sindhi businesses and foster connections within the vibrant Sindhi community.

Our Mission: Sunn Grahak is on a mission to revolutionize the Sindhi business landscape. Our primary objective is to empower Sindhi entrepreneurs, create significant business opportunities, and establish a digital marketplace that serves as a central hub for Sindhi families across India. We aim to simplify the process of connecting consumers with Sindhi vendors, thereby enriching the overall community experience.

The Value We Bring:

At the core of Sunn Grahak lies a commitment to add tangible value to Sindhi businesses and consumers alike. For businesses, we provide a platform to showcase their offerings, connect with a broader audience, and thrive in a digital marketplace. For consumers, we offer a one-stop destination for authentic Sindhi products and services, fostering a sense of community and cultural authenticity.

Founder's Story:

Sunn Grahak was born out of a shared passion for preserving Sindhi culture and facilitating economic growth. Our founder, [Sunita Jeswani & Priya Jeswani], envisioned a platform that not only connects businesses but also becomes a digital bridge uniting Sindhi families scattered across the nation. With a background deeply rooted in the Sindhi heritage, [Sunita Jeswani & Priya Jeswani] embarked on this journey to create Sunn Grahak as a testament to the richness of Sindhi culture.

Meet the Team:

Our dedicated team at Sunn Grahak is fueled by a shared commitment to the Sindhi community's prosperity. Comprising individuals with diverse expertise, our team brings together a blend of technological innovation and cultural understanding. We are united by the belief that technology can be a powerful tool in preserving and propelling the legacy of Sindhi businesses.

Join us at Sunn Grahak and be a part of a community-driven platform that transcends geographical boundaries, connecting Sindhi businesses and consumers with the click of a button.
